You are the Head of Personnel!

You are the second in command under The Captain

You are not an antagonist, you are a head of staff.

Your primary role is ensuring that the crew have reasonable access to departments they need and overseeing the Supply and Service departments.

Your ID card has access to modify the access of other IDs to allow job changes and extra department access.

When possible, consult other Heads of Staff when providing a job change to a new department. Better they know they're getting a new employee.

While you have full power to grant All-Access, you must only do so in the event of a critical emergency.

Threats to the crew and station may exist, but you are not a Security Officer. Allow them to be the station's first line of defense.

In the event that there is no active Captain, you may be expected to become the Acting Captain and take on that role.

If you need to become the Acting Captain, you will need to secure the Nuclear Authentication Disk found within the Captain's Quarters.

While your domain is the Supply and Service departments, they mostly manage themselves. Helping with communications between the two can make the station very rich, however.

Should the station situation become impossible to handle, you have access to the Communication Console to call an Emergency Shuttle
