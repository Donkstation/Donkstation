You are the Captain!

Nanotrasen has entrusted you to keep this station running well.

You are not an antagonist, you are a head of staff.

Your most important personal role is to secure the Nuclear Authentication Disk found within your quarters.

Giving the Pinpointer to another Head of Staff will assist in tracking you and the disk.

Your ID card has access to every part of the station. While you may enter everywhere, try to use discretion with that power.

Threats to your crew and station may exist, but you are not a Security Officer. Allow them to be the station's first line of defense.

Coordination with your Heads of Staff is key to running the station. Your headset has access to all channels should you enable them manually.

Don't attempt to micromanage crew directly; delegate orders through Heads of Staff to the best of your ability.

Should the station situation become impossible to handle, you may use the Communication Console to call an Emergency Shuttle

Also using the Communication Console, you are the one member of the crew that can call an Emergency Response Team or contact Central Command in the event of emergency.

For further information visit: https://wiki.beestation13.com/view/Captain as well as the other guides linked upon that page.


Good luck, Captain, the Station is in your hands.
