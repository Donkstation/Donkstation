You are the Security Officer!

The Head of Security, your superior, expects you to keep the law.

You are not an antagonist, you are Security.

Your main job is to ensure the safety of the crew and that Space Law is upheld.

While stunning and arresting criminals is part of the job, you should do what you can to help innocents. Recovery of injured can help just as much as a clown in a cell.

When out in the field, communication is key. Make sure your fellow officers know your position in the event of suspicious activity. You are a target for Enemies of the Corporation

Work with your team, they each have their own unique skills. Detectives can search an area for evidence while Wardens can replace your equipment and monitor people.

Threats to the crew and station may exist, but consider station alert levels and context when deciding to search or arrest.

When making arrests, make sure that any brig time is equal to the severity of the crime. A minor crime doesn't deserve ten minutes in the box.

If there is a deadly threat to yourself or innocents, lethal force may be required to protect lives.

For further information visit: https://wiki.beestation13.com/view/Space_Law and https://wiki.beestation13.com/view/Security_Officer as well as the other guides linked upon those pages.


Good luck, Security Officer, you might just make it through the day.
