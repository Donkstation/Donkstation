You are the Detective!

The Head of Security has assigned you to this case.

You are not an antagonist, you are Security.

You're here to crack the case and figure out who did it. Leave the arrests to the rest of the team.

Scan objects and doors at the scene of crimes with your Forensic Scanner, which can be used at a short distance away, to gather fibers and fingerprints.

Once you have a set of prints and fibers, use your Security Console to figure out a potential perpetrator to undergo an arrest and search.

While out in the field, communication is key. Make sure your fellow officers know your position in the event of suspicious activity. You are a target for Enemies of the Corporation

Threats to the crew and station may exist, but your position is to only investigate crimes and arrest in the most dire of circumstances.

If there is a deadly threat to yourself or innocents, lethal force may be required to protect lives.
