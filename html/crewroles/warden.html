You are the Warden!

The Head of Security, your superior, expects you to secure his brig.

You are not an antagonist, you are Security.

Your main purpose is to patrol the Security Wing, process prisoners, monitor cameras and to make sure Space Law is upheld.

Working out in the field should only be a last resort, as leaving the Brig unguarded can be very dangerous.

Communication is key, make sure your fellow officers know about suspicuous activity and put an arrest warrant out on any suspects.

Remember, the Armoury is the most armed place on the station. You are a target for Enemies of the Corporation because of this.

Threats to the crew and station may exist, ensure that your team are equipped and able to respond effectively.

When brigging someone, make sure that the punishment is equal to the severity of the crime. A minor crime doesn't deserve ten minutes in the box.

When a large enough threat is caught, you have the power to put them into the Labor Camp or Permabrig. Only the Captain may authorize Execution

If there is a deadly threat to the station itself, distribution of Armoury assets to the crew may be needed.

For further information visit: https://wiki.beestation13.com/view/Space_Law and https://wiki.beestation13.com/view/Warden as well as the other guides linked upon those pages.


Work well, Warden, your team needs you.
