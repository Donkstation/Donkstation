You are the Chief Medical Officer

The Captain is entrusting the health of the station's crew to you.

You are not an antagonist, you are a head of staff.

Your main task is to manage Medical Doctors, Chemists, Virologists and, to a lesser extent, Geneticists and Brig Physicians in keeping the crew alive and well.

Various types of injuries will enter your Medbay, make sure your staff correctly diagnose and treat patients of various species.

There is no shame in keeping reference materials on-hand to refer to while treating a patient..

Making sure that your Chemists create a supply of medicine of various types will ensure swift treatment of patients.

In the event of a Viral Outbreak, isolation of infected is key to prevent a pandemic on the station.

The Virologist or Geneticist may create useful things. Inspect the items in their labs before they are distributed.

Threats to the crew and station may exist, but you are not a Security Officer. Allow them to be the station's first line of defense.

In the event that there is no active Captain, you may be expected to become the Acting Captain and take on that role.

If you need to become the Acting Captain, you will need to secure the Nuclear Authentication Disk found within the Captain's Quarters.

Should the station situation become impossible to handle, you have access to the Communication Console to call an Emergency Shuttle
