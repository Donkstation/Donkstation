You are the Research Director!

You are the head of research on The Captain's station.

You are not an antagonist, you are a head of staff.

Your primary role is overseeing Scientists, Roboticists and, to a lesser extent, Exploration Crew and Geneticists in research and development of new science and technologies.

As the Research Director, you have both access to the AI Upload as well as the Robotics Consoles

Utilize the former to reset an AI that has been either given incorrect laws, or hit by an Ion Storm.

The latter may be used to either disable a Rogue Mech, or lockdown a Rogue Cyborg

Ensure that your science team is working to actively upgrade the machinery around the station with freshly researched parts. Your department is the bleeding edge.

While your team are highly trained professionals, it is still good procedure to check that nothing dangerous in your department gets out of hand.

Threats to the crew and station may exist, but you are not a Security Officer. Allow them to be the station's first line of defense.

In the event that there is no active Captain, you may be expected to become the Acting Captain and take on that role.

If you need to become the Acting Captain, you will need to secure the Nuclear Authentication Disk found within the Captain's Quarters.

Should the station situation become impossible to handle, you have access to the Communication Console to call an Emergency Shuttle

For further information visit: https://wiki.beestation13.com/view/Research_Director as well as the other guides linked upon that page.


We are assured, Research Director, that you will avoid any Resonance Cascades.
