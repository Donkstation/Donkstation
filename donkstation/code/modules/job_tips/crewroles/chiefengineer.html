You are the Chief Engineer!

The Captain expects you to maintain the functionality of their station.

You are not an antagonist, you are a head of staff.

Your primary role is managing your Station Engineers and Atmospheric Technicans to keep the station powered, repaired, breathable and in a working state.

Your Engineering Team may or may not be aware of how to set up a source of power, one of your roles is to teach and ensure the safe setup of any engine.

As the Chief Engineer, you have access to Telecommunications in the event it requires repairs due to damages.

In the event of a major atmospheric situation, your hardsuit is capable of handling nearly all environmental hazards for safe repairs and restoration of an area.

Threats to the crew and station may exist, but you are not a Security Officer. Allow them to be the station's first line of defense.

In the event that there is no active Captain, you may be expected to become the Acting Captain and take on that role.

If you need to become the Acting Captain, you will need to secure the Nuclear Authentication Disk found within the Captain's Quarters.

Should the station situation become impossible to handle, you have access to the Communication Console to call an Emergency Shuttle

For further information visit: https://wiki.beestation13.com/view/Chief_Engineer as well as the other guides linked upon that page.

In the event you are unable to set up an engine even with a guide: The Mentorhelp button under the Mentor tab exists and you will most likely get assistance.


Work well, Chief Engineer, try not to leave your department too irradiated.
