You are the Head of Security!

The Captain is trusting you to maintain law and order on their station.

You are not an antagonist, you are a head of staff.

Your primary role is to manage your team of Security Officers, the Warden and the Detective as they maintain order within the station.

While the Brig Physician and Lawyer are a part of Security, they are not expected to directly make arrests and have their own jobs.

While you may go out into the field, teamwork is important for survival as your ID card and equipment are some of the most powerful on the station. You are a target for Enemies of the Corporation

Threats to the crew and station may exist, but attempt to avoid collateral damage by using non-lethal weapons while possible.

As the Head of Security, you have final say on Arrests, Permabrigging and Labour Camp usage. Only the Captain may authorize Execution and pardon criminals.

In the event that a major, deadly, threat to the station's crew is found, arming Security with lethal weaponry from the Armoury may be required.

In the event that there is no active Captain, you may be expected to become the Acting Captain and take on that role. Allow other Heads of Staff to take first priority over you.

If you need to become the Acting Captain, you will need to secure the Nuclear Authentication Disk found within the Captain's Quarters.

Should the station situation become impossible to handle, you have access to the Communication Console to call an Emergency Shuttle
